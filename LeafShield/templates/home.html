Tips for Controlling Black Spots on Leaves

  • Plant in well-draining soil. Keep your plants healthy by providing regular feedings of organic fertilizer.
  • Remove dead leaves and infected canes from around the plants and discard in the trash.
  • Disinfect your pruners with a household disinfectant after every use.
  • Avoid applying water directly to the leaves. Water the roots instead using a soaker hose.
